## Abstract

This paper proposes a quantum logic gate framework for understanding triosephosphate isomerase enzyme function, with particular focus on how decoherence processes may lead to enzymatic toxicity. The work represents a novel approach to modeling enzyme catalysis through quantum computational principles.

3. **Triosephosphate isomerase**: Focus on this critical glycolytic enzyme as a model system
