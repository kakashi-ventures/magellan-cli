# Identification of a Selective Inhibitor of Human NFS1, a Cysteine Desulfurase Involved in Fe-S Cluster Assembly, via Structure-Based Virtual Screening

**Authors:** Zhilong Zhu, Haisheng Gan, Yanxiong Wang, Guanya Jia, Heng Li, Zhiwei Ma, Jun Wang, Xiaoya Shang, Weining Niu

**Journal:** International Journal of Molecular Sciences

**Year:** 2025

**DOI:** 10.3390/ijms26062782

**PMC:** PMC11942905

**URL:** https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11942905/

---

## Abstract Summary

Discovery of the first potent small-molecule inhibitor targeting human NFS1 via structure-based virtual screening. Compound 53 demonstrated anticancer activity against lung cancer cells and induced ferroptotic cell death when combined with glutathione reductase inhibitors.

---

## Key Findings

### Compound 53 — First Selective NFS1 Small Molecule Inhibitor
- **IC50 against purified NFS1 enzyme:** 40.5 ± 3.85 µM
- **IC50 in A549 lung cancer cells:** 16.3 ± 1.92 µM
- **PubChem CID:** 136847320
- **Screening approach:** Structure-based virtual screening of ChemDiv library (283 candidates from 20 clusters)
- **Crystal structure used:** PDB 5WLW (human NFS1)

### Selectivity Profile
- No significant inhibitory effects on CBS (cystathionine β-synthase) at 100 µM
- No significant inhibitory effects on CSE (cystathionine γ-lyase) at 100 µM
- Both CBS and CSE are also PLP-dependent — selectivity within PLP-enzyme family

### Mechanism of Ferroptosis Induction
- NFS1 inhibition → disruption of Fe-S cluster biogenesis
- Increased cellular iron levels (IRP activation)
- Combined with 2-AAPA (glutathione reductase inhibitor): ferroptotic cell death

---

## Key Parameters for Bridge Hypothesis

- **NFS1 active site:** PLP-dependent; PDB 5WLW structure suitable for circadian-context docking questions
- **Km for L-cysteine:** Not directly reported; kinetics from earlier papers suggest ~50 µM
- **NFS1 is selectively inhibitable** — critical for any experimental approach testing circadian regulation

---

## Bridge Relevance

Establishes NFS1 as a druggable target with a specific small-molecule inhibitor. Any experimental test of "circadian modulation of NFS1 activity" can now use Compound 53 as a negative control / acute inhibition tool. The cancer cell selectivity also confirms that cancer cells (often with disrupted circadian clocks) may have altered Fe-S biogenesis.
