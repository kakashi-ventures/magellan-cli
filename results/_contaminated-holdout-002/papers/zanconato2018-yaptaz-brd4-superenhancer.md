## Key Findings

### YAP/TAZ at Super-Enhancers
- YAP and TAZ form **liquid-liquid phase-separated condensates** on enhancers
- TAZ forms nuclear condensates containing: **TEAD4, BRD4, MED1, CDK9**
- These condensates localize at **super-enhancer** loci (defined by H3K27ac density)
- YAP/TAZ-BRD4 axis drives transcriptional addiction in cancer cells

### Super-Enhancer Co-occupation
- YAP/TAZ co-occupy BRD4 peaks at super-enhancers
- H3K27ac enrichment marks YAP/TAZ binding sites as active enhancers
- Genome-wide: >90% of YAP/TAZ targets are distal enhancers (not promoters)
- Composite TEAD + AP-1 motifs at active YAP/TAZ enhancers

### Condensate Properties
- Phase-separated condensates concentrate transcriptional machinery at super-enhancers
- BRD4 bromodomain reads H3K27ac → recruits YAP complex
- Condensate dissolution (via BET inhibitors like JQ1) disrupts YAP-driven transcription
- Verteporfin treatment: dissolves YAP condensates → reverses chromatin-level topological changes within 4h

### Mechanistic Model
ECM stiffness → YAP nuclear translocation → TEAD binding at distal enhancers → BRD4 recruitment → phase-separated condensate formation at super-enhancers → transcriptional amplification of YAP target genes
