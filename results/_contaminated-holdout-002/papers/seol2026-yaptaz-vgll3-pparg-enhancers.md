# YAP/TAZ-VGLL3 Governs Adipocyte Fate via Epigenetic Reprogramming of PPARγ and Its Target Enhancers

## Key Findings

### YAP/TAZ at Named Enhancers
- YAP/TAZ and their antagonist VGLL3 compete for TEAD binding sites at **PPARγ target enhancers** specifically
- YAP/TAZ binding at these enhancers represses adipogenic gene programs
- VGLL3 displaces YAP/TAZ → enhancer activation → adipogenesis

### Epigenetic Reprogramming
- YAP/TAZ occupancy at PPARγ enhancers maintains H3K27me3 (repressive)
- VGLL3 displacement of YAP/TAZ → H3K27me3 removal → H3K27ac deposition (enhancer activation)
- Demonstrates that YAP/TAZ can act as REPRESSORS at specific enhancers (not just activators)

### Mechanobiology Connection
- YAP/TAZ nuclear localization is ECM stiffness-dependent (via Hippo pathway)
- Stiffer ECM → YAP/TAZ nuclear → repression of PPARγ adipogenic enhancers
- This explains why stiff ECM promotes osteogenesis over adipogenesis in MSCs (YAP represses adipogenic enhancer program)

## Significance for Gap Analysis

- Shows YAP/TAZ have DIRECT effects on specific named enhancers (H3K27ac/H3K27me3 switching)
- The YAP/TAZ → enhancer histone mark axis is real and functional
- **GAP**: This is characterized in MSC adipogenesis; nobody has mapped YAP/TAZ-dependent H3K27me3→H3K27ac switching specifically at mechanoenhancers identified by Cosgrove 2025
- Connects to: stiff ECM → YAP nuclear → mechanoenhancer H3K27ac (written by p300 recruited by YAP) and H3K27me3 removal (via KDM6B, Tayler 2026)
