## Abstract / Key Findings (from search results)

Comprehensive 2024 review of ferroptosis mechanisms and therapeutic targets.

Key findings:

1. **Four main regulatory pathways**:
   - GSH-GPX4 pathway (canonical): cystine import → GSH → GPX4 activity → PLOOH reduction
   - FSP1-CoQ10 pathway: FSP1 reduces CoQ10 → CoQ10H2 scavenges lipid radicals (GPX4-independent)
   - GCH1-BH4 pathway: biopterin synthesis → BH4 as antioxidant
   - DHODH-CoQH2 system: mitochondrial pyrimidine synthesis coupled to CoQ reduction

2. **FSP1 (AIFM2)**: Localized at lipid droplets and plasma membrane. Reduces CoQ10 to ubiquinol outside mitochondria. Ubiquinol acts as lipophilic radical-scavenging antioxidant, preventing lipid peroxidation independently of GSH.

3. **DHODH**: Mitochondrial, couples pyrimidine synthesis to CoQ reduction. Provides inner membrane protection distinct from GPX4 or FSP1.

4. **Iron regulation**: NCOA4-mediated ferritinophagy releases iron to expand labile iron pool → increased Fenton reaction → lipid peroxidation.

5. **Cancer therapy**: Ferroptosis inducers (erastin, RSL3, IKE) show promise in RAS-mutant, triple-negative breast cancer, and platinum-resistant ovarian cancer.
