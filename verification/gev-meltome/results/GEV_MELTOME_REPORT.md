## Methodology

1. Loaded pre-computed Tm values from Jarzab et al. 2020 Supplementary Table S2 (MOESM4)
2. Quality filter: R² ≥ 0.8, converged curve fits only, Tm in [20°C, 100°C]
3. Selected one lysate dataset per species (avoids cell-vs-lysate confound)
4. Fitted GEV distribution via MLE (scipy.stats.genextreme)
5. Bootstrap standard errors (n=1000)
6. Spearman rank correlation of ξ vs OGT across 13 species
